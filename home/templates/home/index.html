About

The Film and Media Council of IIT (BHU), Varanasi started in 2014 by a few cinematography and digital arts enthusiasts, the council ever since promoted the culture of film making and appreciation amongst the student community of IIT (BHU). It includes various generes be it Photography or Cinematography, Creative Design or Animation, Media or Outreach. FMC provides platform where one get to know and experience the thrill of making portfolios be it film making or scripting, photography or designing, anime or outreach. The Council constantly and consistently works throughout the year putting up updates and highlights of all major and minor happenings of the institute via highlights, after movies, glimpses of the events, photographs, posters, merchandise, interviews, monthly and annual release of institute magazines and a lot more to follow.
